Fee Breakdown:

  1. Transaction fee: 2% (By Service provider)
  2. Platform Fee: 5% (Discounts available, refer to the “Discounting” section below for more information)
  3. Promotional Charges:
    • Level 1 (Harnessing Social Media): 4%
    • Level 2 (Reaching out via WhatsApp, SMS, and Voice Calls): 6%
  4. Curation Support: (Optional)

    5. An additional one time fee of 3% for campaigns needing our specialized touch and guidance which will be collected initially while registering the campaign. Discount score is also applicable for this fee as well along with the Platform fee.

Depending on the specifics, your total fee may range from 7% to 20%.

The fees are negotiable for Special and Larger campaigns
